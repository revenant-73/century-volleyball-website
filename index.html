Welcome to Century Volleyball

At Century High School Volleyball, we believe in more than wins—we believe in building athletes who are brave, adaptable, and joyful learners. Our program is grounded in a coaching philosophy that values growth over perfection, accountability without shame, and competition without ego.

We train through the game, not apart from it. Every practice reflects the realities of competition, emphasizing decision-making, adaptability, and learning in context. We challenge our athletes to think, move, and lead—not just execute plays, but solve the game as it unfolds.

Our core values guide everything we do:

  • Joy in the Game: Finding joy in the process, in the struggle, and in every moment we grow together.
  • Effort with Intention: Bringing purpose to every rep, every read, every action.
  • Growth Over Perfection: Embracing mistakes as fuel for improvement.
  • Accountability Without Shame: Holding high standards while supporting one another without judgment.
  • Team Over Ego: Trusting that every role, every voice, and every connection matters.

Success at Century Volleyball is measured not just in scores, but in the culture we build, the confidence we foster, and the growth our athletes carry beyond the court. We strive to create a program where every athlete leaves better than they arrived—as players, as teammates, and as people.

Join us as we play brave, play free, and play the Century way.
