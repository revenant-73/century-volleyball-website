Athletic Registration

All Century volleyball athletes must complete the online registration process before participating in practices or tryouts.

1

Online Registration

Complete your registration on Snap! Manage. Use your existing account if you have one.

Register on Snap! Manage

Note for 9th graders: Your student ID number is the same as middle school if you attended in Hillsboro School District.

2

Physical Examination

An updated physical form must be submitted before the start of the athletic season. Physicals are valid for two years from the examination date.

Download Physical Form
3

Financial Assistance Available

Students who qualify for free and reduced lunch may be granted fee waivers or reductions. If finances are a barrier, please reach out to us.
