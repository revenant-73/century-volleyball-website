Tickets

Game Tickets & Passes

Support Century Athletics by attending our home games! Tickets can be purchased at the door or in advance through our online system.

Ticket Prices

Ticket Type Price Details
Adult Single Game $5.00 General admission for one game
Student Single Game $3.00 With valid student ID
Children (Under 10) Free Must be accompanied by an adult
Senior Citizens (65+) $3.00 With valid ID
Season Pass - Adult $30.00 All home volleyball games for the season
Season Pass - Student $15.00 All home volleyball games for the season
Family Pass $60.00 Admits up to 5 family members to all home games

Purchase Options

In-Person Purchase

Tickets can be purchased at the door before each game. Cash and credit cards accepted. Doors open 45 minutes before game time.

School Office

Season passes and advance tickets are available at the main office during school hours (8:00 AM - 3:30 PM, Monday-Friday).

Online Purchase

For your convenience, tickets can be purchased online through our ticketing partner. Print your tickets at home or show them on your mobile device at the entrance.

Buy Tickets Online

Group Rates

Special rates are available for groups of 10 or more. Please contact the athletic department at least 48 hours in advance to arrange group tickets.

Contact Athletic Department

Booster Club Members

Century Athletic Booster Club members at certain levels receive complimentary season passes as part of their membership benefits. See the Booster Club page for more information on joining and supporting Century Athletics.
